ABOUT DOMINION SCHOOL OF MINISTRY

Dominion School of Ministry is a Bible and leadership development center designed to equip kingdom minded believers for ministry. DSM is a training ground for hungry believers (Apostles, Prophets, Evangelists, Teachers, Pastors, Head of ministries and young ministers) who want to excel in life and ministry and make a big difference in the end time harvest.

We believe that effectiveness in ministry can only happen when believers are equipped. DSM is on a mission to make/raise men and women who are ready to take the world for Christ.

Our facilitators are carefully selected they are among the finest teachers of the word in the body of Christ today with accumulated decades of experience in ministry.

Our course modes are excellently packaged providing a unique approach to teaching of God’s word, practical hand-on ministry experience, impartation of biblical knowledge and wisdom in raising kingdom disciples with maturity, excellence and understanding of the way of the kingdom.

I welcome you to a unique platform of opportunities, impartation, biblical exegesis, insightful teaching and transformation.

God bless you.

Abiodun Oso
Convener

MEET OUR FACILITATORS

Dr Olumide Emmanuel

Pastor Olumide Emmanuel is an anointed servant of God. He is a man of multiple graces, spanning across Spiritual, Business and Relationships. He is a Bible Scholar with over 25 years experience in ministry. He is currently the Overseer of Calvary Bible Church, a Bible-believing Church that has spread her nets all over the world, including Nigeria, United Kingdom, United States, South Africa and so on, preaching the Word of God and winning souls into God’s Kingdom.

Dr Akin John

Started ministry in 1988. Has now been in ministry for years. Pastored 5 denominational churches in the space of 8 years.
Started Church Growth Ministry in 1994 after hearing God said “go and strengthen pastors and support churches to grow and be healthy”. Has held hundreds of conferences across Nigeria, Africa and Europe with combined attendance of over 30, 000 church leaders.
Has written over 80 books that have sold thousands of copies such as ‘Grow the Pastor, Grow the Church’, ‘The Impact-driven Church’, ’22 Laws of Church Growth’. Founded International Institute of Church Growth that has trained over 10, 000 pastors and leaders with attendant growth testimonies.
Has written and produced hundreds of materials on various aspects of church growth, leadership and health that are being used by thousands of church leaders. Has mobilized and equip men to become real men through seminars, resources and tapes.
Has sold thousands of tapes-audio, DVD, CDs that continue to bless and edify thousands of ministers and church leaders.

Dr Niyi Akinsanya

NIYI ADESANYA was ordained as a Pastor at the Daystar Christian Centre in 1998, where he served for 8 years before delving into Itinerant ministry. He preaches averagely every other Sunday in different churches/ denominations globally. While at Daystar Christian Centre, he was awarded twice as the best Pastor in the house fellowship system. He was also in charge of the ministries that were responsible for bringing in the crowd when the church experienced the increase from one Sunday service to four services in ten months. Afterwards, he was moved to the membership ministry which ensuredthat the members were/are retained.
His consulting experience has seen the transformation of multinationals and indigenous business move to profitability, and has also helped churches in experiencing stability, relevance and growth. He is an eloquent speaker, counselor, advocate, teacher, and friend whose messages are full of insight, revelation, truth and the whole counsel of God. He has stayed committed for over 2 decadesin raising commercially astute entrepreneurs via several mentorship platforms. Some of which now set the pace for their industries and contributing a significant quota to the national economy.
Niyi Adesanya has grown to be one of the highest paid speakers in Nigeria and his clients include one out of top three organizations in the major sectors of the economy. He is an unrivalled and renowned Performance Strategist, whose strong business acumen has helped business owners to grow their businesses by leaps and bounds using his innovative MACE procedure template that he developed from his years of leadership management practice. He was awarded Nigerian’s foremost Performance Strategist (Middle East Africa Business Award), Leadership Coach of the year by Top 10 Magazine and awarded the African Prize for Leadership Excellence. He is also the author of six dynamic books on Public Speaking, Leadership, and Peak Performance.
Niyi is a game-changer who is passionate about personal and corporate effectiveness and believes people don't build successful businesses. Systems build successful businesses nevertheless; people must work on and through the systems to build and sustain businesses.

Bishop Akan Lawnrence

Bishop Greatness Akan Lawrence got born again the 23rd of May 1990 at Holy Ghost Storm Tabernacle (The Chapel of Vision Christian University, Uyo). He had served as a leader in various capacities such as Nigerian Fellowship of Evangelical Students (NIFES), University of Port-Harcourt from 1991 till graduation, served as an associate Pastor in Glory Ministry for three years(1993-1996) in Port-Harcourt, Foundation Faith Church worship ministry as the Youth Pastor for seven years (1997-2003), organizing secretary of Nigerian Christian Corpers, Apostle in Destiny International Missions (DIM) in 2012, appointed as the National Presbyter of Destiny International Ministry in 2013 and on the 5th of December, 2019, he was consecrated as a Bishop. In December 2019, he was appointed as Deputy Superintendent of DIM Global
Being an astute teacher of the Word, he was given a position as a senior faculty member of Lead Impact University, Colorado USA where he lectures on Leadership, Capacity Building and Project Management. Bishop Greatness Lawrence headed Ecclesiastical Endorsements for the College of Apostolic Ministers and Bishops Lead Impact alongside His Lordship, have done the work of ministry in many African states
On the 15th of September 2021, the Vision Embassy of Change Global Mission was officially unveiled after 5 years of serving at DIM and several discussions of his vision to Archbishop (Dr) Cletus Bassey. Apart from the church arm of Bishop’s ministry, he runs a teaching ministry Revelation on all social media platforms. His writing ministry is The Lampstand, he has written many books and tracts such as, Fruitfulness, Leadership Matrix and I am not Cain, just to mention a few. Bishop Greatness Lawrence is also graced with music ministry and has produced two worship albums.
Bishop Greatness is not only a man of God with the knowledge of His words, he is a competent Engineer in his field of study who has worked with the Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC) and Nero Communications Ltd. He is a member of professional bodies like Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE), Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) and Harvest International Ministerial Fellowship (HIMF), USA.

Pastor Charles Abu

Charles Abu trained as a Fine Artist from the prestigious Ahmadu Bello University (ABU), Zaria. He has taken several courses on management from several organizations/institutions like the Africa Leadership Forum (ALF), Centre for Management Development (CMD), Daystar Leadership Academy (DLA), and the University of Lagos. Some of his works are documented in the collection, “101 Nigerian Artists” and in private and public collections in Nigeria, South Africa, Europe and the USA.
Charles considers himself as a bridge builder, driven with a passion for people and world events, he manages HANDS Media Coy (Help, Aid, Nurture Development and Skills). Annually he speaks to thousands of young people in conferences and leadership seminars. He has also authored a few books including ‘’Don’t Be Afraid to Start” and “Breakthrough Thinking”.
He and his wife Toyin, both pastor the Empowerment Assembly (TEA), Ikeja, Lagos. They also run the Couples Hearts Ablaze Services (CHAS), an outreach to married couples in retreats, seminars and to singles.

OUR CONVENER

Pastor Abiodun Oso

Abiodun Oso is a lover of God and His kingdom. He is a graduate of Microbiology from the University of Lagos and a holder of MBA from the prestigious Business School Netherlands, Netherlands.
Abiodun Oso is an Evangelist, a Teacher and a Pastor, he is the president of Kingdom Outreach International Ministry, a non-denomination mission outreach to the unreached people and places. Also,he is the setman and Senior Pastor of Dominion Harvest International Ministry, a fast growing family church in Lagos State, Nigeria.
Abiodun is a serial entrepreneur and a business strategist with over 20 years of business venturing in ICT, Real Estate, Food, Media, Tourism, Healthcare and Insurance. He has founded many companies and businesses within Nigeria and outside Nigeria.
He is married to his beautiful wife Philomena Oso and their marriage is blessed with four children.
Abiodun Oso is the Convener and the President of DOMINION SCHOOL OF MINISTRY.
